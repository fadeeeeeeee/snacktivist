the green fine print

Eco-friendly, from the farm to your recycling bin (which you won't need).

Most meal kits call themselves sustainable and ship you a box of plastic ice packs. We built ours to actually disappear.

Fully compostable box

Liners, pouches, and ice packs all break down at home — no separating plastics required.

Carbon-neutral shipping

Every delivery route is offset, and we consolidate orders by region to cut miles driven.

Local, seasonal sourcing

We buy from small regional farms first, shrinking the distance between soil and stove.

Ugly-produce rescue

Perfectly good, slightly odd-shaped produce gets a home in your kit instead of a landfill.
